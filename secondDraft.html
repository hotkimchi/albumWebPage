AEnima is Tool's second full-lengthed album released in 1996. The album was certified triple platinum by RIAA in 2003. The album was dedicated to stand-up comedian Bill Hicks who died 2 years prior to it's release. Tool wanted to raise awarnes about Bill Hick's material and ideas.
